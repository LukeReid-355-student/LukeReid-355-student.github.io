Red
Velvet
Cake

(Southern Style)

Prep + Cook Time:

About 1 hour 10 minutes

Servings:

8–10 slices

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 4 large eggs, room temperature
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons red food coloring
  • 1 teaspoon white vinegar
  • Cream cheese frosting

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F and grease two 9-inch pans.
  2. Whisk flour, cocoa, baking soda, and salt together.
  3. Cream butter, oil, and sugar until light.
  4. Beat in eggs and vanilla until smooth.
  5. Alternate adding dry mix and buttermilk.
  6. Stir in red food coloring and vinegar.
  7. Divide into pans; bake 25–30 minutes.
  8. Cool completely; frost with cream cheese icing.
