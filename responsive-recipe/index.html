Red
Velvet
Cake

(Southern Style)

Prep + Cook Time

About 1 hour 10 minutes

Servings

8–10 slices

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 4 large eggs, room temperature
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons red food coloring
  • 1 teaspoon white vinegar
  • Cream cheese frosting

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F and grease two 9-inch pans.
  2. Mix flour, cocoa, baking soda, and salt in a bowl.
  3. In another bowl, beat butter, oil, and sugar until light.
  4. Add eggs and vanilla, mixing until smooth.
  5. Alternate adding dry mix and buttermilk.
  6. Stir in red food coloring and vinegar.
  7. Pour into pans and bake 25–30 minutes.
  8. Cool completely, then frost with cream cheese icing.
